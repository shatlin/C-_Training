You can expand rows in the Office Scorecard View Web Part to display more detailed views of the data that is associated with the key performance indicators (KPIs) or objectives in the scorecard. Or, conversely, you can collapse rows to show only the KPIs and objectives. For example, a KPI that is used for measuring the sales for grocery stores in a region could be expanded to show the sales for each store, and then collapsed to hide this data.

To expand and collapse rows

  • In Office Scorecard View, you can do any of the following:

    • Click the plus (+) or minus (-) sign next to a row to expand or to collapse that row in the scorecard.

    • On the Office Scorecard View toolbar, click the Expand All button to expand all of the rows in a scorecard.

    • On the Office Scorecard View toolbar, click the Collapse All button to collapse all of the rows in a scorecard.
